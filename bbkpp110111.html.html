Methods of Identification

    

111

 
  Verb Identification      
 
Person : 1st, 2nd, or 3rd
      
 
Number: person and singular
      
 
Tense: Present, Imperfect, Future, Perfect,
      
 
Pluperfect or Future Perfect
      
 
Voice: Active or Passive
      
 
Mood:: Indicatve, Subjunctive, Infinitive,
      
 
Imperative or Participle and why it is in that mood.
      
  Noun Identification      
 
Case: Nom, gen, dat, acc, abl and why it is in that
      
 
case.
      
 
Gender: Masculine, Feminine or Neuter
      
 
Number: Singular or Plural
      
  Adjective Identification      
 
Gender, Number and Case as for a noun, but
      
 
rather than explaining why it is in the case, identify
      
 
what word it modifies.
