Declarative statements:

    

Indirect commands use the subjunctive with ut (neg. nê.
 

Subject is in the accusative.

    

Caesar told Marcus not to go away.
 

Main verb is in the infinitive.

    

Caesar Marcô imperâvit nê abîret.
 
He said that Caesar had come.
    
NB: iubêre is an exception and requires acc. + inf.
 
Dîxit Caesarem vênisse.
