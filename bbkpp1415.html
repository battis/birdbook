        A noun is i-stem if:
        1) Nominative and genitive have same number of syllables and
        the nominative end in -is or -es, or
        2) Nominative ends in -s and the stem ends in Ž2 consonents, or
        3) It is a neuter noun whose nominative ends in -e, -al, or -ar!
