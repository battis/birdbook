Ut clauses

    

103

 
  1) Purpose clauses answer the question, 'Why?'      
 

Jason went to Colchis to obtain the golden fleece.

      
 

Iâsôn ad Colchidem îvit ut vellis aureum obtinêret.

      
         
  Negative is nê = 'that...not,' 'lest'      
 

Jason went to Colchis that he might not die.

      
 

Jason went to Colchis lest he die.

      
 

Iâsôn ad Colchidem îvit nê morerêtur.

      
         
  2) Result clauses answer the question, "How?"      
 

Jason was so brave that he killed the dragon.

      
 

Iâsôn tam fortis erat ut dracônem occîderet.

      
         
  Negative is ut...nôn      
 

Jason was so brave that he did not fear the dragon.

      
 

Iâsôn tam fortis erat ut dracônem nôn timêret.

      
         
  3) Indirect Command clauses answer the question, "What?"      
  (also called Substantive purpose)      
 

Pelias ordered Jason to go to Colchis.

      
 

Peliâs Iâsonî imperâvit ut ad Colchidem îret.

      
         
  Negative is nê      
 

Pelias ordered Jason not to remain in Greece.

      
 

Peliâs Iâsonî imperavit nê in Graecia manêret.
