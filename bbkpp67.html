nominative singular, genitive singular, gender
      
 
puella, puell|ae, f., girl
      
         
 
Singular
      
nominative puella The girl (subject)      
genitive puellae The girl's, of the girl      
dative puellae to or for the girl      
accusative puellam The girl (object)      
ablative puellâ FWIB the girl      
         
 
Plural
      
nominative puellae The girls (subject)      
genitive puellârum the girls', of the girls      
dative puellîs to or for the girls      
accusative puellâs the girls (object)      
ablative puellîs FWIB the girls      
         
  Almost all first declension nouns are feminine.
