Uses of the Genitive
        1) The genitive is used to express possession.
       

templum Augustî - 'The temple of Augustus'
         
        2) Genitive of the whole (aka partitive genitive):
       

nimium vînî - 'to much (of) wine'
       

nihil bonî - 'nothing (of) good'
